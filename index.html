Update on Training 2025

Very Imporant Information

As we are an outside user group of HMCS TECUMSEH, we are subject to the use of the facilities based on the higher needs of our host unit, the Canadian Armed Forces, and sometimes various government bodies.

We have been informed of some dates in the New Year where we have been restricted from the use of the facilities. These restrictions may affect the main building only and some the entire property. We are revising our training plan for the new year. You will see a combination off site and facility run activities. Some will require parental pick up and drop off at various locations and possibly at times different from normal, unit training. These revisions are a work in progress. I suggest you don't look to far ahead than a month at a time at this point. The revisions affect each month through the end of the training year. We will communicate any changes, (and changes to the changes) in the Weekly Update and verbally on parade if possible. Ensure you read these updates fully for complete information.

Note also that you will see some Area or Regional Directed Activities overlapping with unit level activities. If you are signed up to attend the ADA's and/or RDA's, you are expected to attend those and you are excused from the unit level activity.

We appreciate your understanding as we navigate these unfamiliar waters. Rest assured, it is the goal of the staff, with the support of the Navy League, Calgary Branch, to continue to provide fun and engaging training activities for our cadets.
